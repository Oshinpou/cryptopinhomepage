CryptoPin Finance - FAQ

1. What is CryptoPin Finance?
CryptoPin Finance is a decentralized social platform that enables users to engage with crypto content and earn rewards.
2. How do I promote my posts using CryptoPin?
Users can promote posts by making crypto payments, which increases their visibility and reach on the platform.
3. What cryptocurrencies are supported?
CryptoPin supports BNB, BTC, ETH, and the native CPT token, with plans to expand to other networks.
4. How does the staking mechanism work?
Users can stake CPT tokens and earn rewards through yield farming and staking pools.
5. What is the total supply of CPT tokens?
The total supply is capped at 1 billion CPT tokens to ensure scarcity and long-term value.
6. How are transaction fees utilized?
A portion of transaction fees is burned to reduce supply, while the rest supports liquidity and platform growth.
7. Can I trade NFTs on CryptoPin?
Yes, CryptoPin has a built-in NFT marketplace where users can trade and showcase unique assets.
8. Is my wallet secure on CryptoPin?
Yes, CryptoPin implements advanced encryption and multi-layer security protocols to protect user wallets.
9. How does governance work in CryptoPin?
Holders of CPT tokens can participate in decentralized governance through voting on community proposals.
